Group 5: Meetings At Sea

By Jamie Faber (2754619), Sebastiaan Kruize (2626979) and Loek van Steijn (2601124)

About:

In this project 2.6TB of data is used to display vessel meetings at sea. The data entails AIS messages, which contain identification information of the vessels. As the sea is vast, it is difficult to monitor all meetings. This can lead to off the radar illegal activity. Our goal is to showcase relevant meetings. To do this the meetings were displayed on a map using Leaflet. On this website meetings for each day are visible on the Days page and meetings for each month are visible on the Months page. Below the maps is a Sankey graph, which visualizes the flag state of the meeting vessels.
