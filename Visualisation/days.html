Use the slider to select the preferred day (Move the slider to make the data visible). The viewable days consist of the month February 2017. Click on the clustered markers to zoom in on the cluster or click on the ship marker to examine meeting information. The vessel flag state is the country under which jurisdiction the vessels are and the duration shows the length of the meeting in hours. The map gives a detailed view of the locations of the meetings. As you can see, most meetings are coastal and in Europe.
Daily Meeting Map:

Below the map is a Sankey diagram, the shown date can be selected. Hover the diagram for further information or zoom in with the control panel to get a better view of specific countries. The flag states of the meeting vessels are displayed with a line connecting the countries. The circles are meetings between vessels from the same country. As can be seen in the graph the majority of the meetings are in Europe, namely The Netherlands.
